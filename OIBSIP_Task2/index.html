A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. He was a scientist and science administrator at the DRDO and ISRO and was involved in India's civilian space programme and military missile development efforts. He thus came to be known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.

Awards & Honours

Padma Bhushan, 1981

Padma Vibhushan, 1990

Bharat Ratna, 1997

Braun Award, 2013
